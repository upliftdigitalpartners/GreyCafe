Our Story

A cozy corner
in Queens.

GREY Cafe is a women-owned neighborhood spot — originally known as Browny — where you can slow down with a beautifully brewed cup from our La Marzocco machine, settle in with your laptop, or catch up with friends over freshly made sandwiches and pastries.

We care about sustainability, inclusivity, and doing things the right way — from plastic-free packaging and fair-trade organic coffee to partnering with Too Good To Go. Our space features gender-neutral restrooms, full wheelchair accessibility, and a post-it note wall where visitors leave messages.
